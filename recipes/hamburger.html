Hamburger

Juicy cheeseburger with bacon.

This recipe is about clasic cheesburger with a twist. We added some crispy bacon the make everything %100 percent better

Ingridents

  • Buns
  • 150g of meat
  • Bacon of your choice
  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Make a patty
  2. Toast buns
  3. Cook the patty
  4. Add cheese on top of the patty and let it melt
  5. Take the patty and put it in the bun
  6. Add lettuce and Tomato and close the bun
